Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

Carol Royse Team ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and submit an application to join our real estate team.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via our website includes:

Personal Data

When you submit an application through our website, we collect personally identifiable information, such as your:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Professional information (homes closed, real estate experience)

2. How We Use Your Information

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the website to:

  • Evaluate your application to join Carol Royse Team
  • Contact you regarding your application status
  • Send you text messages and emails about career opportunities
  • Assess your qualifications as a real estate agent
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

3. Text Message Communications

By providing your phone number and checking the opt-in box on our application form, you consent to receive text messages from Carol Royse Team regarding:

  • Your application status
  • Interview scheduling
  • Career opportunity updates
  • Information about joining our team

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. You can opt out at any time by replying "STOP" to any text message. For help, reply "HELP" or contact us at the information provided below.

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share information we have collected about you in certain situations. Your information may be disclosed as follows:

By Law or to Protect Rights

If we believe the release of information about you is necessary to respond to legal process, to investigate or remedy potential violations of our policies, or to protect the rights, property, and safety of others, we may share your information as permitted or required by any applicable law, rule, or regulation.

Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your information with third parties that perform services for us or on our behalf, including CRM systems, email delivery, and text message services.

5. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide to us, please be aware that despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and no method of data transmission can be guaranteed against any interception or other type of misuse.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of text messages by replying "STOP"
  • Opt out of emails by clicking the unsubscribe link
